#### Multi-participant Sessions

there are currently issues in how a session and group keyboard is handled, when multiple users joined.
what i want is the following:
- if the conversation on one participants times out doe to inactivity, he should get a notification that the conversation was closed due to inactivity and the conversation should always be cancled for him, so that there is no ongoind conversation anymore.
- if the conversation times out for one participant it should not affect the others. 
- if a conversation times out it should remain suspended in the background if there were modifications done (in case nothing was done, it should just close). After 10min of inactivity also a suspended conversation should be closed.
what currently happens wrongly is:
- conversations are not always correctly cancled if it times out while another user is still active
- if a conversation times out for one user, it will also close for the other users, even if they are still active (and worst of all it doesnt even cancle the conversation for them). Instead the session and keyboard should stay open for the others.
- suspended conversations stay active indefinetly and the user handeling of participants can get messed up.
